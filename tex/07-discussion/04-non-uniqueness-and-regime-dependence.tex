\subsection{Non-uniqueness and regime dependence}
  \label{subsec:non-uniqueness-and-regime-dependence}

  Spectral reconstruction is intrinsically non-injective.
  Distinct relational configurations may yield indistinguishable
  effective metrics within a given admissible regime.
  Geometric descriptions are therefore approximate and
  regime-dependent.

  This non-uniqueness reflects a structural feature
  of emergent geometry rather than a theoretical inconsistency.
