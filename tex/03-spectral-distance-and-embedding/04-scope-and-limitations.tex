\subsection{Scope and limitations}
  \label{subsec:scope-and-limitations}

  The constructions developed here are purely kinematic.
  No assumptions on dynamics, temporal ordering, or causal structure are made.
  Their sole purpose is to reconstruct effective geometric notions from spectral
  data of relational connectivity.

  Continuum limits, curvature reconstruction, and comparison with known
  geometric solutions are addressed in subsequent sections.
