Dear Editor,

Please find attached our manuscript entitled “Spectral Reconstruction of Effective Geometry from Relational Laplacians”
submitted for consideration as a Paper in your journal.

This work presents an original and self-contained framework for reconstructing effective geometric structures from
purely relational and spectral data. Starting from a relational Laplacian defined without reference to a background
manifold, we show how continuum-like geometric behavior can emerge as an effective and regime-dependent description,
and we identify intrinsic limits beyond which such a geometric interpretation necessarily breaks down.

The manuscript combines analytical arguments with numerical investigations, including a robust spectral ratio emerging
under refinement, and clarifies the operational status of geometric operators in discrete-to-continuum settings.
The results are intended to be of interest to researchers working on spectral geometry, relational approaches to
spacetime, and foundational aspects of gravity and geometry.

This submission reports original research that has not been published elsewhere and is not under consideration by any
other journal. All authorship and affiliation information is included in the manuscript, and the submission is intended
for single-anonymous peer review.

Thank you for your consideration. We believe this work fits well within the scope of the journal and we look forward to
your response.
