\subsection{Non-circular dependency structure}
  \label{subsec:non-circular-dependency-structure}

  The construction is strictly hierarchical:
  \[
    d^{C}
    \;\Longrightarrow\;
    \bar{\chi}
    \;\Longrightarrow\;
    w(\bar{\chi}),\,K(\bar{\chi})
    \;\Longrightarrow\;
    d^{W}
    \;\Longrightarrow\;
    \chi_{\mathrm{eff}}.
  \]

  The coarse-graining neighborhoods defining $\bar{\chi}$ depend
  only on $d^{C}$ and are therefore $\chi$-independent.
  The effective geometry enters only through $d^{W}$, whose weights
  depend on $\bar{\chi}$, eliminating instantaneous feedback.

  The procedure is purely kinematic and introduces no dynamical
  equation for $\chi$ or $\chi_{\mathrm{eff}}$.
