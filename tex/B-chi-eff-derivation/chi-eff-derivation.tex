\section{Derivation of \texorpdfstring{$\chi_{\mathrm{eff}}$}{χeff} from Relational Observables}
  \label{app:chi-eff-derivation}

  The effective field $\chi_{\mathrm{eff}}$ provides an operational
  description of $\chi$ in regimes admitting a stable projected
  geometry. Its construction must avoid circular dependence on
  the emergent metric structure.

  To ensure this, we employ the two-level scheme introduced in
  Appendix~\ref{subsec:combinatorial-vs.-weighted-distance}:
  the combinatorial distance $d_{ij}^{C}$ defines coarse-graining
  neighborhoods, while the weighted distance $d_{ij}^{W}$
  encodes the effective geometry only after $\bar{\chi}$ has been
  constructed.
